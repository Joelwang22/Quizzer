16  •   A   sound   argument is a   valid   argument whose   premises are all true.  Soundness = (1) validity + (2) all premises being true.  Both conditions need to be met for an argument to be sound.  •   A   valid   argument :  Its premises and conclusion are connected in such a way that…  if   the premises are all true, the conclusion must be true as well .
